Know about us

The Non Academic Staff Union (NASU) is a trade union that represents non-academic workers in institutions and organizations across Nigeria. Founded in 1978, when several unions were merged by the Government of Nigeria. NASU is affiliated with the Nigerian Labour Congress and has over 260,000 members. NASU FCE Special Oyo is one of the active long-established branches of NASU, with a dynamic membership possessing various attributes, including being results-oriented and development-driven.The Union has a team of agile and pragmatic Executives who are highly innovative, empathic about members welfare and development. A team being led by Comr. Badmus Tolulope Adekunle, a Political Scientist and an academic enthusiast.
